							President Abdel Fattah Al Sisi met Secretary General World Tourism Organistion  UNWTO  Taleb Rifai Wednesday in the presence of Egyptian Minster of Tourism Hisham Zaazou 
Al Sisi stressed upon the importance of tourism as a main source of income for Egypt and local authorities have spared no efforts in securing tourists  A statement issued by the presidential office reported that Rifai praised security standards applied in ports and airports by Egypt 
Egypt is putting constant effort into attracting tourists but the business is currently endangered by the security situation  A Russian plane crashed over Sinai on    October       killing over     passengers abroad amid unconfirmed reports suggesting a bomb on board caused the crash  Egyptian investigations are still ongoing 
Before that  a military raid accidentally killed a group of Mexican tourists in the Bahariya oasis  claiming they had entered a restricted zone
