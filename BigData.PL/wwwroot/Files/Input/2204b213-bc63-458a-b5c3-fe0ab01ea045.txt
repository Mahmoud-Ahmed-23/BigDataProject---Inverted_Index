							Al Marasem International for Urban Development has accelerated its growth in Egypt s real estate sector with a planned investment of EGP  bn across a series of new projects  The company is currently in the process of evaluating two prime land plots one spanning     feddans in East Cairo and another     feddans in West Cairo signaling the start of a significant expansion phase aimed at bolstering its development pipeline 
These planned acquisitions reflect Al Marasem s broader strategy to solidify its presence in high demand areas while continuing to deliver high quality developments  In       the company reported sales of approximately EGP     bn from its projects in New Cairo  Sheikh Zayed  and Ras El Hekma  With confidence in Egypt s improving macroeconomic climate and sustained real estate demand  Al Marasem is targeting even stronger sales performance in      
Since its entry into the real estate market in       Al Marasem has built a reputation for large scale  integrated communities  Its flagship project  Fifth Square  located in the heart of New Cairo s Fifth Settlement  covers     feddans and comprises       residential units distributed across     apartment buildings  along with     villas  townhouses  and twin houses  The development also features     hotel serviced units and a retail mall with     stores across a built up area of         square meters  Complemented by an administrative building and an array of social and recreational amenities  the project adheres to modern architectural standards and urban planning principles 
To date  Al Marasem has delivered over       residential units at Fifth Square  with the project nearing     occupancy and full completion expected by mid       The company leveraged its in house construction division and secured financing through Banque Misr  with accelerated loan repayment already underway as part of its financial risk management approach 
Expanding beyond Fifth Square  Al Marasem recently launched two major developments  Mar Ville in New Sheikh Zayed  covering     feddans and introduced in late       and Mar Bay in Ras El Hekma  a     feddan coastal project unveiled in summer       These new projects bring the company s total land portfolio close to       feddans  reinforcing its position as one of Egypt s leading private developers 
In addition to its real estate ventures  Al Marasem has diversified its operations through the establishment of six specialized subsidiaries  These entities span sectors including industrial construction  trading  hospitality  property management  and specialized contracting  Among them is ICPM  which acts as the company s industrial arm  as well as Marota Hotels Management  a hospitality partnership with Rotana  In       the company is directing EGP  bn in investments toward projects managed by these subsidiaries  supporting its broader vision for integrated  multi sector growth 
Al Marasem s comprehensive expansion strategy underscores its commitment to long term investment in Egypt s real estate market  combining land development  financial discipline  and operational diversification to meet growing demand and reinforce its leadership in the sector
