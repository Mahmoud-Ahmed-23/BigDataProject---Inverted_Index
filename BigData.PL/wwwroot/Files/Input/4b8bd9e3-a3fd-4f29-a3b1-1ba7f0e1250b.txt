							Khaled Abdel Ghaffar  Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Health and Population  received a high level delegation from the French Development Agency  AFD  to discuss ways to enhance joint cooperation in the healthcare sector  monitor the progress of ongoing projects  and explore future collaboration opportunities 
 nbsp 
At the outset of the meeting  Abdel Ghaffar expressed his appreciation for the AFD       s role in supporting Egypt s healthcare system  emphasizing the importance of the partnership in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals and improving the quality of healthcare services provided to citizens 
 nbsp 
Hossam Abdel Ghaffar  the official spokesperson for the Ministry of Health and Population  stated that the meeting addressed several areas of current collaboration  including a project to support healthcare services and strengthen the capacities of primary healthcare units  as well as a program to bring in French experts to share knowledge in healthcare planning and health economics 
 nbsp 
The discussion also focused on building the capacities of Egyptian medical staff through specialized training programs in hospital management  monitoring  and evaluation  These include sending medical teams to participate in exchange and training programs within French hospitals to gain hands on experience in various clinical specialties 
 nbsp 
The spokesperson noted that the meeting also touched on a new project in cooperation with the European Union aimed at upgrading Egypt s health units in line with the standards of the General Authority for Healthcare Accreditation and Regulation  GAHAR   as part of the government s broader plan to improve the quality of primary care services 
 nbsp 
Moreover  the two sides explored the possibility of deploying French experts to provide on the job training in governorates involved in the second phase of Egypt s Universal Health Insurance System 
 nbsp 
Abdel Ghaffar added that the talks included discussions on shared health challenges faced by both countries  particularly the high neonatal mortality rates  The two parties explored avenues for scientific cooperation and the exchange of expertise in this critical area 
 nbsp 
For their part  representatives of the French Development Agency reaffirmed their commitment to strengthening cooperation with the Ministry of Health  underscoring their dedication to supporting Egypt s healthcare development projects in alignment with national priorities 
 nbsp 
They also expressed their keenness to play an active role in the healthcare sector as a cornerstone of sustainable development and pledged full support for the implementation of universal health coverage plans
