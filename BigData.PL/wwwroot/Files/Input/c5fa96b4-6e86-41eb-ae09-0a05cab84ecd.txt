                                                                Social Science is a broad field of study that covers a wide range of disciplines  including anthropology  economics  geography  history  political science  psychology  and sociology  Social Scientists study the social and cultural aspects of human life  including our relationships with each other  our institutions  and our environment Social Science is important because it helps us to understand the social experience  By studying social and cultural phenomena  we can learn more about ourselves  our relationships with society  and the world around us  Social Science research is used to inform a wide range of decisions  including public policy  business strategy  and social program development Social ScienceBranches of Social ScienceSocial scientists study a wide range of topics  including Branches of Social ScienceAnthropology  The study of human societies and cultures  past and present Economics  The study of how people produce  distribute  and consume goods and services Geography  The study of the Earth s surface  as well as the distribution of natural resources and the effects of human activity on the environment History  The study of past events and their impact on the present Political science  The study of government and politics  including the institutions  processes  and behaviors associated with political power and authority Psychology  The study of human behavior and mental processes Sociology  The study of human social behavior  especially the groups and institutions that shape it Divisions of Social Science There are mainly four branches of Social Science mentioned below
