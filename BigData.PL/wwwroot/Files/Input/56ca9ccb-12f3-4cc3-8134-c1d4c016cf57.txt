                What is Narrative PR Summit
                                
            

                    
          
            
The Narrative PR Summit is a platform designed to reintroduce Egypt as a young  progressive nation with a strong position in the global economy  The summit ultimately aims to attract FDI and encourage interest in Egypt as a destination for leisure and investments

                Al Rawi Storyteller Award
                                
            

                    
          
            
A prestigious recognition that celebrates outstanding storytellers who capture and highlight the essence of Egypt       s cultural richness and modern developments  This award aims to showcase the unique blend of Egypt s historical heritage with its forward thinking approach to innovation and progress  It highlights how Gen Z storytellers can play a vital role in shaping perceptions of Egypt  drawing attention to its hospitality  welcoming spirit  and dynamic future  The award winners will be announced during Narrative Summit with   shortlisted recepients for each category
